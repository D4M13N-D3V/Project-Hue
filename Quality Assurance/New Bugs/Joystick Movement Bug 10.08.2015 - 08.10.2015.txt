Bug Information 
- When The Left Joystick is moved in the following directions at quick successions (two quick taps in the mentioned directions should do it), the character moves on it's own. 

Bug cause
- Moving the Joystick twice in the following directions:
	East
	West
	South East
	South
	South West

The directions: North East, North and North West are not affected by this bug however.
